Post 2

Meetup Information

I went to the NewTech meetup on Thursday, May 17th.

People I Met

Jonathon Yarbor, CEO of Nodecraft, and Dave Noreen, CEO of Save Away 401k.

What I Learned

I learned about some new tech startups in Denver and across the country. Five or six CEO's spoke about their company's, some of which were company's like Nodecraft, Save Away 401k, and BizEatz. Nodecraft is a company that allows PC gamers to easily rent game servers from the company rather than go through the trouble of making one themselves. Save Away 401k is a retirement savings company, and BizEatz is a company that delivers food to business travelers on the go. I also learned that a company called Xero is moving from San Francisco to Platte Street and is looking to hire multiple developers.

Questions I Have

What kind of company is Xero and will they still be hiring in October? If so, what kind of developers?
